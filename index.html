Located in Park Slope, Pacific Tavern is a purveyor of fine American dining from the esteemed Chef Billy Lang.

photos by nicole franzen

Pacific Tavern opened in May 2010 and is located inside the Smith Hotel Brooklyn. Since then, Chef Lang has been serving both quality interpretations of classic dishes and dar ing new ventures into the uncharted territories of food.
